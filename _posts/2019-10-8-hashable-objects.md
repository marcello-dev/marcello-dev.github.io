| Class       | Immutable          
| ------------- |-------------|
| bool | yes      
| int  | yes 
| float | yes           
| tuple | yes      
| str | yes
| frozenset | yes
| bytes | yes
|complex | yes
| set | no
| dict | no
| list   | no 
| byte array | no
